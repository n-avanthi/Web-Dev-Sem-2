ABOUT US

We are authorized by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), Ministry of Civil Aviation to do hot air ballooning in India on a commercial basis. All our pilots have thousands of hours of flying experience; our team comprises internationally licensed Balloon Pilots from Europe, USA, Australia & India. We adhere to all maintenance and security guidelines laid down by Civil Aviation Authorities of India. Our balloons are sourced from the best manufactures in the world.

Fly like a bird with the Bangalore hot air balloon ride and see the sun coming up. Start your day with this amazing experience that you will never forget for the rest of your life.

Enjoy a bird's eye view of the city and fly up to 250 feet above the ground for 10 minutes. Starting anytime between 5:30 AM - 7:30 AM. The hot air balloon in Bangalore activity is taken care of in the presence of trained and experienced guides who have all the safety measures in place during the flight.

A flight can take a maximum of 7 people at a time. Feel your adrenaline pumping through your veins while you experience unparalleled pleasures of your life.
