ABOUT US!

Welcome to Guessto! Our mission is to promote inclusivity and accessibility by encouraging everyone to learn sign language and providing support to the speech-impaired and hearing-impaired community.

Our Vision

At Guessto, we envision a world where communication barriers are broken down, and everyone has the opportunity to communicate freely and effortlessly. We believe that learning sign language is a powerful way to bridge the gap between individuals who are hearing-impaired, speech-impaired, or have difficulties with spoken language.

Why Sign Language?

Sign language is a visual-gestural language that utilizes hand movements, facial expressions, and body language to convey meaning. By learning sign language, we can create a more inclusive society, enabling better communication and understanding between individuals with different abilities.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a platform that not only educates people about sign language but also raises awareness about the challenges faced by the speech-impaired and hearing-impaired community. Through our quiz, we aim to make the learning process interactive, engaging, and enjoyable for users of all ages and backgrounds.

Join Us

We invite you to be part of our journey in making sign language accessible to all. By participating in our quiz and sharing it with others, you're helping us spread awareness, encourage learning, and promote inclusivity. Together, we can make a difference and empower individuals with diverse communication needs.
